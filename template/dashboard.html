Shipping Policy

We want your order to arrive quickly, safely and with transparent delivery expectations. The following sections explain delivery timing, customs, import charges, and our working-day fulfillment policy.

UK Domestic Orders

We provide next-day delivery for orders placed within the United Kingdom. Once your order is processed and dispatched, it will normally arrive the following working day. Processing times may vary slightly depending on order volume and payment verification.

International Orders (Europe only)

Orders shipped to European destinations are dispatched within 10–15 working days. This timeframe refers to order processing and shipment departure; actual delivery may take longer depending on carrier transit time, customs clearance, and local postal services.

Delivery Estimates

When your purchase is processed, we present the total amount payable and an estimated delivery date. This estimate is based on processing times, shipping method, and destination. Dates are projections and may change due to factors outside our control.

Import Duties & Taxes

International deliveries may be subject to duties, taxes, or other charges imposed by the destination country. If these charges apply, we will indicate whether they are payable at the time of purchase or upon delivery by the carrier or customs authority.

Customs Advice

If import fees are payable on delivery, we recommend contacting your local customs office for information on likely charges, procedures, and required documentation. Customs rules and rates differ between countries and may change without notice.

Working Days Only

Deliveries are carried out on working days only, typically Monday through Friday. Orders are not delivered on weekends or public holidays unless explicitly stated by the selected carrier or shipping option.
